About

Labels are great. How useful it is to neatly pack and organize our thoughts. Yet, the trade off is the risk of boxing the mind into those set boundaries.

About me, huh? An engineer? A writer? An explorer? A student? Yes, all true. But how I fear limiting myself within those, or any, labels.

I created this website as a space for showcasing finished and ongoing work. That's what this website is about — and ultimately, that's what I'm about.

Is there a better way to describe someone other than that which they put out into existence?

Jaime Danguillecourt
